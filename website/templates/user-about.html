What is StarCraft 2?


StarCraft II is a sequel to the real-time strategy game StarCraft, announced on May 19, 2007, at the Blizzard Worldwide Invitational in Seoul, South Korea. It waseventually set to be released as a trilogy. StarCraft 2 become free-to-play since November 14, 2017, and since then, there are a lot of new players and old players who have been playing StarCraft from across the globe coming to play this game. Simplest way to explain how the gameplay work is player will choose between 3 races which is Protoss (Ancient, noble warriors with a golden armada and advanced psionic technology), Terran (Resourceful humans with varied technology and plenty of attitude) and Zerg (A ferocious swarm of merciless beasts that devour worlds). Players will then construct their base and gather minerals and vespine gas to build production structures and upgrade their technology. After that, players will spend minerals and vespene gas to amass a powerful and distinctive army. Finally, players will deploy their forces with creativity and finesse to overcome their opponents.

StarCraft 2 Training System

StarCraft 2 Training System is a new system which can train players by playing against A.I that is more unpredictable, more resilient, and more effective at their strategy. Players who use this system could also analyze their performance against our A.I to see which area they are lacking and improve themselves. With the StarCraft 2 Training System, players will experience various benefits, such as effective training in playing StarCraft 2 and wider view on their performance as a player.

Our A.I

We create our A.I using python-sc2. An easy-to-use library for writing AI Bots for StarCraft II. Our ultimate goal is to create an A.I that could plays StarCraft 2 at a pro-level. With limited resources, we could only rely on limited data to train our A.I but with enough training, we could reach our goal.
